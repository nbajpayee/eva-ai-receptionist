#### **Section 1: Are Medical Spas Covered by HIPAA? (300 words)**
- **Yes, if:**
  - You bill insurance for any treatments
  - You accept HSA/FSA payments
  - You're affiliated with a medical practice
  - You store patient medical records
- **Key point:** Even aesthetic-only practices often qualify due to medical record keeping

#### **Section 2: What is Protected Health Information (PHI)? (300 words)**
- **18 PHI identifiers:**
  1. Names
  2. Phone numbers
  3. Email addresses
  4. Medical record numbers
  5. Account numbers
  6. IP addresses
  7. Dates (appointment dates, birth dates)
  8. ... (list all 18)
- **Why it matters for scheduling software:**
  - Every appointment contains name + phone + date = PHI
  - Call recordings with names = PHI
  - SMS confirmations with appointment details = PHI
- **Implication:** Your scheduling/communication tools MUST be HIPAA compliant

#### **Section 3: Business Associate Agreement (BAA) Requirements (400 words)**
- **What is a BAA?**
  - Legal contract between covered entity (you) and service provider (vendor)
  - Required for ANY vendor that handles PHI on your behalf
- **Who needs to sign a BAA:**
  - ✅ Scheduling software providers
  - ✅ AI receptionist services
  - ✅ Email marketing platforms (if they store patient contact info)
  - ✅ SMS notification services
  - ✅ Cloud storage providers (for patient records)
  - ❌ Your CPA (doesn't handle PHI)
  - ❌ Office cleaning service (no PHI access)
- **What a BAA must include (per HHS guidelines):**
  1. How PHI will be used/disclosed
  2. Safeguards to protect PHI
  3. Subcontractor provisions
  4. Breach notification procedures
  5. Termination clauses
  6. Indemnification (who's liable if breach occurs)
- **Red flags:**
  - Vendor refuses to sign BAA → Not HIPAA compliant
  - BAA template missing required clauses → Incomplete protection
  - Vendor claims "HIPAA compliant" but won't provide BAA → Run away
- **Eva AI example:** We provide signed BAA with all medical spa accounts, covering voice, SMS, email channels
- Internal link: [Download Sample BAA Template](/hipaa#baa-template)

#### **Section 4: Technical Safeguards for Software Vendors (400 words)**
- **Encryption requirements:**
  - **In transit:** TLS 1.2+ encryption (like HTTPS for websites)
  - **At rest:** AES-256 encryption for stored data
  - **Backups:** Encrypted backups with access controls
- **Access controls:**
  - Role-based permissions (admin vs. front desk staff)
  - Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required
  - Automatic session timeouts
  - Audit logs (who accessed what, when)
